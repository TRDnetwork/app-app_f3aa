Dawn Brew

Crafted in silence. Roasted by hand. Arriving Summer 2026.

Origin Story

Born from early mornings and quiet rituals, Dawn Brew began as a simple pursuit: to create coffee that honors the moment before the world wakes. Sourced from small farms where care meets craft, each bean is selected for its story as much as its flavor.

Brewing Philosophy

We believe coffee should be felt, not rushed. Our roasting process is slow and intentional, guided by scent, sound, and instinct. No automation. No shortcuts. Just heat, time, and attention — transforming raw beans into something warm, complex, and deeply human.

Coming to Karnal

This summer, we bring our craft to the heart of Karnal. A quiet corner with wooden counters, soft light, and the steady hum of grinders. A place to begin the day with intention — and a cup that tastes like care.
